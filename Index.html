About Me

I am an undergraduate Geology and Mining student at the University of Barishal and a Research Fellow at the Barishal University Research Society (BURS). I actively engage in GIS-based research covering LULC dynamics, NDWI, NDVI, air quality, and climate resilience.

Using tools like ArcGIS, ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Python, and Google Earth Engine, I analyze environmental challenges and collaborate with experts to develop data-driven solutions that promote sustainability and address climate issues.

Field Work Experiences

2023 – 2025

2023, Sitakund: I conducted a detailed 3-day field survey in Sitakund’s coastal hills, focusing on lithological and geomorphological mapping. We collected rock and sediment samples, documented erosion patterns, fault lines, and structural features. I led the team in sample collection and ensured accurate field notes. This work provided crucial baseline data for understanding coastal dynamics and sharpened my observational skills.

2024, Kaptai & Rangamati: Over multiple days in Kaptai and Rangamati, I performed stratigraphic and tectonic mapping of complex hill terrains. We identified landslide-prone areas and collected soil and rock samples for lab analysis. I guided my teammates on sampling techniques and data recording, ensuring quality and consistency. This fieldwork enhanced my understanding of regional geomorphology and structural geology.

2025, Cox’s Bazar: In Cox’s Bazar, I undertook comprehensive fieldwork examining coastal and deltaic geomorphology. We collected marine and river sediment samples, mapped sediment deposition zones, and analyzed fluvial processes. I managed field logistics and ensured rigorous data quality control while mentoring junior members. This work deepened my expertise in coastal geology and sediment dynamics.
